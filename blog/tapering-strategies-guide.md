  <h1 style="font-family: 'Cinzel', serif; color: #D4AF37;">Tapering Strategies: A Practical Guide</h1>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); font-size: 1.1rem; line-height: 1.8; margin: 2rem 0;">
    Whether you're taking a tolerance break or discontinuing use, tapering prevents withdrawal symptoms. Here's the protocol used by clinical researchers.
  </p>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">The 10-Day Taper Protocol</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    For moderate users (5-15g daily):
  </p>

  <div style="background: rgba(0,0,0,0.3); padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1.5rem 0;">
    <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
      <li><strong>Days 1-3:</strong> Reduce by 25% (e.g., 15g → 11g)</li>
      <li><strong>Days 4-6:</strong> Reduce by 25% again (11g → 8g)</li>
      <li><strong>Days 7-8:</strong> Reduce by 33% (8g → 5g)</li>
      <li><strong>Days 9-10:</strong> Reduce by 50% (5g → 2.5g), then stop</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">Withdrawal Symptom Management</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    Kratom withdrawal is typically milder than opioid withdrawal (FDA's own data). Symptoms peak at 2-3 days and resolve within a week:
  </p>

  <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
    <li><strong>Rhodiola rosea:</strong> 300mg 2x daily for fatigue/mood</li>
    <li><strong>Magnesium glycinate:</strong> 400mg nightly for RLS/anxiety</li>
    <li><strong>Black seed oil:</strong> 1 tsp daily for general withdrawal</li>
    <li><strong>Exercise:</strong> Cardio accelerates alkaloid clearance</li>
  </ul>

    <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin-top: 0.5rem;">

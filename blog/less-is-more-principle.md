  <h1 style="font-family: 'Cinzel', serif; color: #D4AF37; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
    Responsible Use: The "Less Is More" Principle
  </h1>

  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); font-size: 1.1rem; line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
    It's counterintuitive, but it's the most important rule in kratom pharmacology: <strong>higher doses often produce worse effects</strong>. Understanding the bell-curve dose response can save you from nausea, the "wobbles," and rapid tolerance buildup.
  </p>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin: 3rem 0 1rem;">The Bell Curve Explained</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
    Kratom's active alkaloids, primarily mitragynine, exhibit a <strong>biphasic dose response</strong>:
  </p>

  <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
    <li><strong>Low doses (1-3g):</strong> Stimulation, mood elevation, focus via adrenergic and dopaminergic activity</li>
    <li><strong>Moderate doses (3-5g):</strong> Analgesia, anxiolysis via mu-opioid receptor activity</li>
    <li><strong>High doses (5g+):</strong> Sedation, nausea, dizziness—the "wobbles" (nystagmus)</li>
  </ul>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,0,110,0.1); border: 1px solid rgba(255,0,110,0.3); padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 2rem 0;">
    <strong style="color: #ff006e;">⚠️ What Are "The Wobbles"?</strong>
    <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.8); margin-top: 0.5rem;">
      Involuntary eye movement (nystagmus), nausea, and dizziness caused by excessive dose. Usually resolves in 2-4 hours. Prevention: stay under 5g, especially with potent strains like Super Green or White Horn.
    </p>
  </div>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin: 3rem 0 1rem;">Tolerance Prevention</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
    The "less is more" principle also applies to frequency. Daily high-dose use leads to rapid tolerance and dependence. Strategies:
  </p>

  <ol style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
    <li><strong>Strain rotation:</strong> Alternate vein colors (white → green → red → yellow) to prevent receptor downregulation</li>
    <li><strong>Days off:</strong> 1-2 non-consecutive days per week resets tolerance</li>
    <li><strong>Agmatine sulfate:</strong> NMDA antagonist supplement that potentiates kratom and slows tolerance (500mg 30min prior)</li>
    <li><strong>Minimum effective dose:</strong> Find your lowest working dose and stick to it</li>
  </ol>

  <div style="background: rgba(0,245,212,0.1); border: 1px solid rgba(0,245,212,0.3); padding: 2rem; border-radius: 12px; margin-top: 3rem;">
    <h3 style="color: #00f5d4; margin-bottom: 1rem;">The "Sweet Spot" Method</h3>
    <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.6;">
      Start at 2g. Wait 45 minutes. If effects are insufficient, add 0.5g. Repeat until desired effects achieved. <strong>Do not exceed 5g in a single session.</strong> Your "sweet spot" is the minimum dose that produces full effects—usually 2.5-3.5g for most users.
    </p>

  <h1 style="font-family: 'Cinzel', serif; color: #D4AF37;">Kratom Bans in Thailand vs. Indonesia</h1>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); font-size: 1.1rem; line-height: 1.8; margin: 2rem 0;">
    Two Southeast Asian nations. Two opposite approaches to the same plant. Thailand's prohibition-and-reversal versus Indonesia's export-dependent tolerance offers lessons for global drug policy.
  </p>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">Thailand: Ban and Regret (1943-2022)</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    The 1943 Kratom Act criminalized the tree to protect <strong>state opium monopoly revenue</strong>—not public health. The result:
  </p>

  <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
    <li>Traditional use driven underground</li>
    <li>10,000+ arrests annually by 2010s</li>
    <li>Prison overcrowding for possession</li>
    <li>No reduction in actual use</li>
  </ul>

  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    In <strong>August 2022</strong>, Thailand decriminalized kratom nationwide—citing the failure of prohibition and economic potential. Within months, regulated kratom cafes opened, and traditional <em>quid</em> preparation returned.
  </p>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">Indonesia: The Export Paradox</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    Indonesia produces <strong>95% of American kratom</strong>—yet domestic use is minimal and regulation is complex:
  </p>

  <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
    <li>Kratom not illegal, but not officially "legal" either (regulatory gray zone)</li>
    <li>2021 proposal to ban kratom exports (threatening global supply)</li>
    <li>Domestic consumption discouraged (cultural stigma)</li>
    <li>Export revenue: estimated $130M+ annually to Indonesian economy</li>
  </ul>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,193,7,0.1); border: 1px solid rgba(255,193,7,0.3); padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 2rem 0;">
    <strong style="color: #ffc107;">The 2021 Export Ban Threat</strong>
    <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin-top: 0.5rem;">
      In 2021, Indonesia's National Narcotics Agency (BNN) proposed scheduling kratom—citing "abuse potential" despite minimal domestic use. The AKA and Indonesian farmer cooperatives successfully lobbied against the ban, arguing economic harm to 250,000+ farming families.
    </p>
  </div>

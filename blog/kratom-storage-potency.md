<div style="max-width: 800px; margin: 0 auto; padding: 2rem;">
  <h1 style="font-family: 'Cinzel', serif; color: #D4AF37;">Kratom Storage: Keeping Powder Potent</h1>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); font-size: 1.1rem; line-height: 1.8; margin: 2rem 0;">
    Mitragynine degrades. UV light, oxygen, and heat break down alkaloids over time. Here's how to preserve your investment—and avoid the disappointment of weak, stale kratom.
  </p>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">The Enemies of Potency</h2>
  
  <table style="width: 100%; color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); border-collapse: collapse; margin: 2rem 0;">
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2);">
      <th style="text-align: left; padding: 1rem; color: #D4AF37;">Threat</th>
      <th style="text-align: left; padding: 1rem; color: #D4AF37;">Effect</th>
      <th style="text-align: left; padding: 1rem; color: #D4AF37;">Solution</th>
    </tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);">
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">UV Light</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Rapid alkaloid degradation</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Opaque containers</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);">
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Oxygen</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Oxidation of mitragynine</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Airtight seals, vacuum bags</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);">
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Heat</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Accelerated chemical breakdown</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Cool storage (<70°F)</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Moisture</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Mold, bacterial growth</td>
      <td style="padding: 1rem;">Desiccant packets</td>
    </tr>
  </table>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">The Ideal Storage Setup</h2>
  
  <p style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 1.8;">
    For long-term storage (1-2+ years):
  </p>

  <ol style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
    <li><strong>Mason jars</strong> with tight lids (glass is inert, no plastic off-gassing)</li>
    <li><strong>Vacuum-sealed bags</strong> for bulk storage (removes oxygen)</li>
    <li><strong>Desiccant packets</strong> (silica gel, food-grade)</li>
    <li><strong>Cool, dark location</strong> (basement, closet—not garage or kitchen)</li>
    <li><strong>Freezer</strong> for 2+ year storage (vacuum-seal first to prevent condensation)</li>
  </ol>

  <div style="background: rgba(255,193,7,0.1); border: 1px solid rgba(255,193,7,0.3); padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 8px; margin: 2rem 0;">
    <strong style="color: #ffc107;">Signs of Degraded Kratom</strong>
    <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); margin-top: 0.5rem; line-height: 1.8;">
      <li>Loss of green color (turns brown)</li>
      <li>Stale, hay-like smell (vs. fresh "green" aroma)</li>
      <li>Weak effects at normal doses</li>
      <li>Visible mold (discard immediately)</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <h2 style="color: #D4AF37; font-family: 'Cinzel'; margin-top: 3rem;">Shelf Life Expectations</h2>
  
  <ul style="color: rgba(255,255,255,0.9); line-height: 2;">
    <li><strong>Poor storage</strong> (plastic bag, sunlight): 3-6 months</li>
    <li><strong>Good storage</strong> (sealed jar, dark cabinet): 1-2 years</li>
    <li><strong>Optimal storage</strong> (vacuum-sealed, frozen): 3+ years</li>
  </ul>

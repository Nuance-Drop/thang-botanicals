The Opportunity

The botanical market is flooded with mass-produced, low-quality product sourced through exploitative supply chains. THANG is different.

We've eliminated the traditional 4-5 layers of middlemen by partnering directly with generational farmers in Southeast Asia. This isn't just ethical—it's highly profitable.

Competitive Advantage

Direct relationships mean we control quality from seed to sale while paying farmers 40% above market rates. The result? Exclusive access to strains no other US vendor can offer, at margins that traditional importers can't match.

Sustainability & Ethics

Our farmers practice traditional agroforestry—no clear-cutting, no pesticides, no chemical fertilizers. These botanicals grow in their natural rainforest habitat, harvested by hand at peak maturity.

This isn't marketing fluff. We visit our partner farms quarterly. We know the families. And we're building the first fully traceable supply chain in the industry.
